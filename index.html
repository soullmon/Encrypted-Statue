HOW TO CREATE YOUR STATUE
  1. Select and drag a stone from the gallery to the Podium area (or click to spawn).
  2. Remove the Stone and arrange it → the stone immediately attaches to the previous stone.
  3. If the stone fails on the podium → the stone falls and you lose.
  4. The Analyze button is active after at least 1 stone is attached, and can only be used once.
  5. Create the work as aesthetically pleasing as possible and name your statue.
